About AgriPro

More than a crop.
A mission.

Agricultural Professionals Ltd., known publicly as AgriPro, is a Jamaican agricultural organisation based in Kingston. We design, manage and deliver projects that advance Jamaica's agriculture, develop next-generation agricultural leaders, stimulate economic development, and improve quality of life and well-being.

Alongside our project and development work, we cultivate premium Scotch Bonnet Peppers for both the local Jamaican market and international export. Our produce reflects the same standard of care and intention that drives everything we do.

We are a business that believes in the transformative power of agriculture, not just as an industry, but as a vehicle for real, lasting change in Jamaican communities and beyond.

Questions & Answers

The questions we hear most often.

Quick answers to what clients and partners usually ask. If yours isn’t here, just email us.

What does Agricultural Professionals Ltd. do?
Agricultural Professionals Ltd. (AgriPro) designs, manages and delivers projects that advance Jamaica's agriculture. This includes crop production, agricultural leadership development, community economic development, and overall quality-of-life improvement through agricultural initiatives. We also cultivate premium Scotch Bonnet Peppers for both local and overseas markets.
What is a Scotch Bonnet Pepper and why does AgriPro focus on it?
The Scotch Bonnet is Jamaica's signature pepper, known for its distinctive fruity heat and deep cultural roots in Caribbean cuisine. It is highly sought after locally and internationally. AgriPro cultivates it as our flagship crop because it represents a strong commercial opportunity, a genuine connection to Jamaican agricultural identity, and a product we can deliver with consistency and pride.
Do you sell Scotch Bonnet Peppers to local buyers in Jamaica?
Yes. We supply Scotch Bonnet Peppers to the local Jamaican market. Whether you are a business, distributor, or individual buyer, contact us at admin@agriproltd.com or call +1 (876) 358-6221 to discuss your requirements and availability.
Do you export Scotch Bonnet Peppers to overseas markets?
Yes. AgriPro cultivates Scotch Bonnet Peppers for both local and overseas markets. If you are an international buyer or importer seeking authentic Jamaican Scotch Bonnet Peppers, reach out to us at admin@agriproltd.com to discuss supply terms, volume, and availability.
What types of agricultural projects does AgriPro manage?
AgriPro designs and delivers a range of agricultural projects, including crop production and farm management, agricultural leadership and capacity-building programmes, economic development initiatives, and community-focused agricultural programmes. Our approach is full-cycle: we design, manage, and deliver.
How does AgriPro support the development of agricultural leaders?
Developing the next generation of Jamaican agricultural leaders is a core part of our mission. We are actively developing programmes that provide training, mentorship, and practical experience for individuals looking to build careers and businesses in agriculture. Stay connected on our social channels for updates.
Where is AgriPro located?
Our office is at Suite 18, 13 Haining Road, Kingston, Jamaica. You can reach us by phone at +1 (876) 358-6221 or by email at admin@agriproltd.com. Follow us on Instagram @agripro_ltd for updates.
Does AgriPro work with other organisations or government bodies?
AgriPro is open to partnerships, collaborations, and engagements with organisations, institutions, and government bodies that share our commitment to advancing Jamaican agriculture. Contact us to discuss how we can work together.
Are you open to consulting or project management engagements?
Yes. If you have an agricultural project, initiative, or challenge you need help designing or managing, AgriPro is available to discuss consulting and project management engagements. Get in touch at admin@agriproltd.com with a brief overview of your project.
How do I stay updated on AgriPro’s activities and announcements?
Follow us on Instagram at @agripro_ltd and on Facebook at AgriPro Limited. We share cultivation updates, project announcements, and agricultural news across our social channels.
